About Me & Data Feminism Project

Positionality Statement

Coming from an area where reproductive healthcare access is limited, the #WeCount project speaks to issues of abortion volume in conservative areas.

Research Focus

After the 2022 Dobbs SCOTUS decision struck down the national right to abortion access, there has been a lack of comprehensive data about changes in reproductive healthcare. The #WeCount initiative gathers this data from providers and publishes it online.
